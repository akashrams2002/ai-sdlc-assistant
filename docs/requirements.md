# Requirements

- Project Idea: Online Food Delivery App
- 1. The app must allow users to create and manage their accounts, including profile information, order history, and payment details.
- 2. The app should enable users to browse and search for restaurants, view menus, and place orders for delivery or pickup.
- 3. The app must integrate a secure payment gateway to facilitate online transactions and support multiple payment methods.
- 4. The app should provide real-time order tracking, including order confirmation, preparation, and delivery status updates.
- 5. The app must have a rating and review system, allowing users to rate and review restaurants, dishes, and overall experience.
